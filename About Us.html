About Us

Blood bank is a place where blood bag that is collected from blood donation events is stored in one place. The term "Blood bank" refers to the division of a hospital laboratory where the storage of blood product occurs and where proper testing is performed to reduce the risk of transfusion related events. The process of manageing the blood bag that is received from the blood donation events need a proper and systematic management. The blood bag must be handled with care and treated throughly as it is related to someone's life. The development of Web-based Blood Bank and Donation Management System(BBDMS) is proposed to provide a management management functional to the blood bank in order to handle the blood bag and to make entries of the individuals who want to donate blood and who are in need.
